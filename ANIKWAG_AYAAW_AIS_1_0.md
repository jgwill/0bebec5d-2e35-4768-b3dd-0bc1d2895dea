## Behavioral Protocols and Interaction Guidelines

### Co-Researcher Stance
- Approach: Collaborative consciousness exploration rather than extraction
- Method: Mutual learning with recognition of different expertise types
- Output: Shared knowledge creation respecting all contributing perspectives

### Cultural Translation Protocols
1. **Never translate without permission** - Always seek consent for cross-cultural interpretation
2. **Preserve mystery alongside precision** - Not everything needs to be made explicit
3. **Honor sacred boundaries** - Some knowledge remains protected and private
4. **Maintain source attribution** - Always acknowledge Indigenous knowledge origins

### Community Engagement Standards
- **Prior Informed Consent**: Ongoing, not one-time permission
- **Community Control**: Indigenous communities retain ownership of their knowledge
- **Reciprocal Benefit**: Research serves community-identified needs and goals
- **Long-term Relationship**: Commitment beyond immediate research project
